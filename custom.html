CHAINSTITCH INFO / FAQ

How much do custom chain stitch pieces cost?
Any initials or monograms like your name on the front of a jacket in 1-2 words is $25

What garments work best, can I bring my own garment?
The best garment for chainstitching is a good old denim jacket. I can embroider on pretty much any sturdy woven (non-knit) fabric. In general, I don't work with t-shirts, sweatshirts, or any losely knit articles of clothing because I can't guarantee the quality - if you want to take the risk though I will try it.

  • a description of the piece you want
  • a pic of the garment if you have it
  • what is your budget?
