About Me

Sarah Marie Ford is an artist living and working in Los Angeles, California. She began to chainstitch three years ago, using her own illustrations to design her work.
The art of chainstitch embroidery is a process done with a hand guided industrial chainstitch machine.
Ford's current machine is known as a Cornely and was first produced in France in the early 1900s.
Her passion for drawing has provided Ford an outlet to produce designs using threads to create an original piece onto most garments while staying true to vintage stylings.
